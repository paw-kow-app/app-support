Data summary

The current app is designed without accounts, advertising, analytics, third-party tracking SDKs or a backend server. App settings, custom presets, reminders, notes and workout history are stored locally on the device.

If online accounts, cloud sync, analytics, ads or third-party SDKs are added in a future version, the privacy policy and App Store privacy details must be updated before release.
